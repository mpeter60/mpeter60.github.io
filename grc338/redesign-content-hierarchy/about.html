Our Italian Restaurant in SLO

Gennaro's Grill and Garden serving authentic Italian cuisine is located in a lovely Old Victorian, buit in 1904 at 450 Marsh Street in the heart of San Luis Obispo on the Central Coast.

Enjoy our Italian cuisine in any one of our six rooms ~ The Main Dining Room, the Fireplace Room, the Green Room, the Sun Room, the Bar Lounge or in our outdoor Garden. We have a full bar service to compliment your dining experience.

We will be open on Mother's Day for a special brunch, and also on Saturday, June 15th as well as on Sunday June 16th for lunch.

We also accommodate meetings or private dinner parties in our more private room - The Fireplace Room, and design special menus that incorporate your personal requests. Please contact Gennaro directly at 805-782-9999 to coordinate your arrangements.

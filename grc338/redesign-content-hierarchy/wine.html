Gennaro's Grill and Garden is in the heart of wine country, including San Luis Obispo, Paso Robles, Edna Valley and Arroyo Grande. In our extensive wine list, we feature wines from local vineyards as well as Napa, Sonoma and Mendocino regions. We also have a large selection of Italian wines as well as a large selection of library wines, both imported and domestic.
